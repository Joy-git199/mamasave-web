Product Overview

MamaSave is a wearable maternal health monitoring system designed to reduce maternal and neonatal mortality rates in rural and underserved communities. Built around a compact, low-cost embedded system, MamaSave continuously monitors vital signs such as body temperature, heart rate, and uterine pressure using reliable sensors like the DS18B20, MAX30100, and DF9-40. The system is physically embedded into a soft, adjustable belt worn around the waist, ensuring maximum comfort and minimal interference with daily activities. Unlike traditional hospital-based monitoring solutions, MamaSave brings healthcare closer to the user by enabling home-based, continuous observation. Sensor data is processed in real time using an ESP32 microcontroller and transmitted wirelessly via Bluetooth to a mobile app. This mobile interface acts as both a visual dashboard and alert system, helping expectant mothers, caregivers and health workers identify warning signs early and act quickly in case of emergencies. The wearable is especially tailored for areas with limited infrastructure. By operating offline and on battery power, MamaSave ensures uninterrupted functionality in regions where electricity and internet access may be inconsistent. The project was designed with cost-effectiveness, simplicity, and cultural acceptance in mind, making it accessible to families and clinics across low-income areas. Beyond its technical functionality, MamaSave is more than a gadget, it’s a life-saving companion. It supports mothers in monitoring their pregnancy safely from home, helps health centres extend their reach, and contributes to Uganda’s national goals of reducing preventable maternal deaths. MamaSave represents a smart fusion of engineering and empathy, tackling a real-world problem with sustainable, student led innovation.

Discover Our Solution

The Urgent Problem

There is an urgent and critical need for an accessible, low-cost, real-time, and location-aware solution that can effectively monitor the health of expectant mothers and newborns, triggering timely emergency interventions to save lives.

Our Innovative Solution

MamaSave is an embedded system-based wearable and alert solution designed to address these critical gaps by providing continuous maternal and newborn health monitoring.

Wearable Health Monitor

Designed as a waist belt for pregnant mothers. Equipped with sensors to monitor: Maternal heart rate, Blood Oxygen , Body temperature, Uterine contractions (labour detection) .

Real-time Emergency Detection

System continuously analyzes sensor data. Detects dangerous patterns such as Abnormal blood Oxygen levels, abnormal heart rate, or onset of labour. Automatically triggers an alert when thresholds are exceeded.

Alert System

Sends emergency messages with the mother's condition and GPS location. Notifies local midwives, health centers, family contacts or ambulance drivers. Works with mobile data for reliability

GPS-Based Location Sharing

Mobile in-built GPS module pinpoints the mother’s location during an emergency. Helps responders quickly locate the individual in remote areas.

Our Technology Stack

Core Hardware

  • Microcontroller: STM32 / ESP32 / Arduino Nano
  • Sensors: MAX30100 (heart rate & SpO2), BP sensor, temperature sensor, vibration sensor (for contractions)
  • Power: lithium-ion battery, charging circuit

Design & Software Approach

  • Physical Design: Custom perforated board for robust connections.
  • Casing: 3D printed enclosure integrated into the belt.
  • Power Efficiency: Optimized for low power consumption, utilizing ESP32 deep sleep.
  • User Interface: Mobile application (front-end developed) for data visualization and interaction, utilizing phone's GPS.
